Overview

The BlizzExpansionPanelComponent is a component that provides a collapsible panel with a trigger and content. It allows users to expand or collapse the panel to show or hide its content.

Usage
<bzz-expansion-panel>
  <bzz-expansion-panel-trigger>Panel Trigger</bzz-expansion-panel-trigger>
  <bzz-expansion-panel-content>Panel Content</bzz-expansion-panel-content>
</bzz-expansion-panel>
  • bzz-expansion-panel-trigger - the component that represents a header element of the expansion panel. It is the element that users interact with to expand or collapse the panel. This component typically contains text, buttons, or icons that provide a visual cue and indicate the state of the panel. By default, it contains a chevron arrow representing whether panel is expanded or collapsed.
  • bzz-expansion-panel-content - the component that represents a content of the expansion panel. It is the section that is shown or hidden when the panel is expanded or collapsed, respectively. This component can contain any type of content, such as text, images, or other components, to provide additional information or functionality related to the panel.
