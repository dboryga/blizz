Expansion Panel with Custom Indicator

  <bzz-expansion-panel-trigger>Panel Trigger</bzz-expansion-panel-trigger>
  <bzz-expansion-panel-indicator>
    <!-- Indicator Content -->
  </bzz-expansion-panel-indicator>
  <bzz-expansion-panel-content>Panel Content</bzz-expansion-panel-content>
</bzz-expansion-panel>
Grouped Expansion Panels

In most cases it is recommended to use Accordion component, to easily achieve grouped expansion panels. 

<bzz-expansion-panel group="groupId">
  <bzz-expansion-panel-trigger>Panel 1</bzz-expansion-panel-trigger>
  <bzz-expansion-panel-content>Panel 1 Content</bzz-expansion-panel-content>
</bzz-expansion-panel>

<bzz-expansion-panel group="groupId">
  <bzz-expansion-panel-trigger>Panel 2</bzz-expansion-panel-trigger>
  <bzz-expansion-panel-content>Panel 2 Content</bzz-expansion-panel-content>
</bzz-expansion-panel>
